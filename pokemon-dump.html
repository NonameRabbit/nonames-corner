Random Pokemon Dump

Useless trivia and facts about Pokemon.

JAPANESE NAMES

Passimian's Japanese name is the longest of all when written in romaji (Nagetsukesaru - 13 letters).

If you combine the consonants from Koromori (Woobat) and the vowels from Pururiru (Frillish) , you get Kurumiru (Sewaddle) .

Dangoro (Roggenrola) is an anagram of Goronda (Pangoro) .

Klinklang's Japanese name is Gigigiaru, which is the only Japanese Pokemon name with 3 of the same syllable in a row.

There are currently 3 Pokemon who have triple vowels in their Japanese names: Yukinooo (Abomasnow ) , Meeekuru (Skiddo ) , and Runaaara (Lunala ).

SIZES

The heaviest non-Gigantamaxed Pokemon in existence (Celesteela , Primal Groudon , and Cosmoem - 2,204 lbs each) weigh less than a Fiat 500.

Gigantamax Alcremie is the tallest of all Pokemon. At 98'5", they're almost double the size of Wailord, the previous record holder.

Garbodor , Passimian , and Type:Null are all 6'3".

Mantine is heavier than Registeel .

Mudsdale weighs exactly twice as much as Snorlax .

MISCELLANEOUS

Lunala and Hoopa Confined are the only Pokemon who have a double weakness to their own type.

All Eeveelutions have base stat totals of 525.

Stunfisk , Oricorio , and Komala have higher BSTs than 75% of Johto Pokemon, including legendaries.

No one knows what Registeel eat.

There are no spider-based Pokemon with 8 legs.

Despite not being Flying-types, Genesect and Volcarona are the only Bug-types that can learn Fly.

Gastly and Wooper can both learn Ice Punch despite neither one having arms. Black Kyurem literally has arms made of ice, but cannot learn Ice Punch!
